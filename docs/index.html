Problem Statement

In large parking areas, drivers spend significant time searching for empty parking spots. This leads to congestion and inefficiency. Existing solutions are expensive or require deep learning.

Our Solution

We propose a lightweight classical computer vision system that detects parking occupancy using CCTV feeds without deep learning.
